Mickey Mantle

Mickey Mantle

Topps 1952 Baseball Trading Card: $12.6 million

Mickey Mantle Topps 1952 Card (#311) – $12.6 Million: The famous Topps 1952 Mickey Mantle card was part of one of the first modern baseball card sets. It is rare because it was released late in the season, so fewer were bought, and many unsold cards were later thrown into the ocean by the Topps company. Mantle was also a very popular player, so many kids who owned his card kept it instead of trading it away. Today, less than 1% of graded cards from that set are Mantles, and only a few exist in top condition.
