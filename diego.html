Diego Maradona

1986 World Cup Jersey

$9.3 million – Diego Maradona is remembered as one of the greatest players to ever step onto a soccer field, famously leading Argentina to victory in the 1986 World Cup.

One moment from that tournament remains unforgettable: the \"Hand of God\" goal. In a controversial play during the quarterfinal match against England, Maradona used his hand to guide the ball past goalkeeper Peter Shilton. He later scored a remarkable second goal to secure a 2–1 victory and propel Argentina toward the championship.

The jersey worn during that historic match was sold for $9.3 million at a Sotheby’s auction in 2022, less than two years after Maradona’s passing.
